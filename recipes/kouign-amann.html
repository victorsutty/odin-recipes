Kouign Amann

Ingredients

  • 550 g bread flour
  • 15 g of Guérande Salt
  • 10 g yeast
  • 500 g melted butter
  • 450 g caster sugar

Steps

  1. Recipe for two large cakes serving 8people for 25 individual cakes.
  2. Mix the flour, salt, yeast, 20g of melted butter
  3. Add 350g of water to obtain an homenous texture.
  4. Leave pastry to rest (rise) for approx. 30 mins at room temperature (20-22°C).
  5. Roll out the pastry, place a 450g square of butter in the centre and fold the pastry over it. Leave to cool for 20 mins in the fridge.
  6. oll out lengthways and fold into three as you would to make puff pastry (one fold).
  7. Leave for an hour in a cool place in cling film.
  8. Repeat the operation, adding the caster sugar. Fold the pastry once. Leave to stand again for 30 mins in a cool place. Roll out the pastry to a 4mm thickness and cut into 8cm squares.
  9. Place the pastry into circular moulds that have been greased and dusted with sugar, folding the corners in to the centre and then place these on a greased, sugared non-stick tray.
  10. Leave to rise for approx. 1hr30 at 24-26°C, during which the pastry should increase by half its volume. Bake for 40 mins at 180°C.
